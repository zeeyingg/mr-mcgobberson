Cat

Born a pig and named Cat, the young fellow experiences life's joys and sorrows... He was neglected at a random carnival shortly after he was born. Little did he know, DFMC was nearby playing some carnival games. When DFMC won, she rescued Cat and brought him home. At first, Cat wanted to escape, but was slowly moved by DFMC's APCS code. Now, Cat continues their journey as DFMC's beloved ducky and coding buddy.
